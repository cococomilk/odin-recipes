Ama's Fish/Vegetable Sauce

This looks soooo good. Also from my late Ama.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp Butter
  • 3 tbsp Flour
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • ¼ tsp Pepper
  • ½ cup Evaporated milk
  • ½ cup Water or stock
  • 4 pcs Egg yolks, beaten
  • ¼ cup Butter, melted
  • 3 tbsp Lemon or calamansi juice

Steps

  1. Blend butter and flour in a 16cm sauce pan.
  2. Add salt and pepper and stir until mixture is smooth and bubbly.
  3. Remove from fire and add milk and water or stock gradually.
  4. Bring to a boil for one minute while stirring constantly.
  5. Remove from fire, add egg yolks, melted butter and lemon juice.
  6. Pour over fish or vegetables just before serving.
