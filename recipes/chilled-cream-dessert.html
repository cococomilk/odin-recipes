Chilled Cream Dessert

This one is taken from my late grandmother's many cookbooks.

Ingredients

  • 1½ tbsp Unflavored gelatine
  • 1¼ cup water
  • 1 cup Cream
  • 2 pcs Eggs, separated
  • ¾ cup Sugar
  • 2 tsp Vanilla
  • ½ cup Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Soften gelatine in ¼ cup water.
  2. Heat remaining water with cream in a sauce pan.
  3. Beat egg yolks and sugar together and add gradually to hot mixture.
  4. Cook until mixture coats a spoon.
  5. Remove from heat, add gelatine and stir until dissolved.
  6. Place sauce pan over ice water, when mixture begins to thicken, add vanilla.
  7. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites and whipped cream.
  8. Pour into mold and chill until firm. Serve with fruit!
