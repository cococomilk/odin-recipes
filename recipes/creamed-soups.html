Ama's Creamed Soups

Another one from my late grandmother's many cookbooks.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 2 tbsp Flour
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • ¼ tsp Pepper
  • 1 cup Evaporated milk
  • 3 cup Broth
  • ½ cup Vegetable or cooked meat
  • Garnish - choice of one: whipped cream, grated cheese, cheese balls, cheese puffs, popcorn, parsley croutons

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a 20cm sauce pan and add flour.
  2. Add seasonings.
  3. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly until smooth and bubbly.
  4. Stir in vegetable/cooked meat and broth.
  5. Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally.
  6. Remove from heat. Gradually stir in evap milk. Heat to a serving temperature.
  7. Pour in individual soup bowls and garnish.
